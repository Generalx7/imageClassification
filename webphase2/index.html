Risks Detection and Assessment

  • Explore the different types of skin cancer and learn about the latest diagnostic and treatment options.

    The Seven types of Skin Cancer :

    ✔ 1 - Basal Cell Carcinoma (BCC)
    → Diagnosis: Usually diagnosed through a skin biopsy.
    → Treatment: Options include surgical excision, cryotherapy, radiation therapy, and topical medications like imiquimod.

    ✔ 2 - Melanoma (MEL)
    → Diagnosis:diagnosed through skin biopsy and may involve imaging tests like CT or MRIs to determine the extent of cancer.
    → Treatment: May involve surgical excision chemotherapy , Radiation therapy.

    ✔ 3 - Actinic keratosis (AKIEC)
    → Diagnosis:Typically diagnosed through a skin biopsy.
    → Treatment:May include surgical excision,radiation therapy and immunotherapy.

    ✔ 4 - Melanocytic nevus (NV)
    → Diagnosis:Through a combination of skin biopsy and blood tests.
    → Treatment:Based on the type and stage of lymphoma.




  • ✔ 5 - Dermatofibroma (DF)
    → Diagnosis:Involves a skin biopsy and imaging tests and blood tests.
    → Treatment:May include surgery,radiation therapy immunotherapy therapy.

    ✔ 6 - Vascular lesion (VASC)
    → Diagnosis:Through a skin biopsy,involve tests to determine the extent of the tumor.
    → Treatment:Involves surgical excision, Mohs surger, targeted therapy.

    ✔ 7 - Benign keratosis (BKL)
    → Diagnosis:Through a skin biopsy and may involve imaging tests to assess for spread.
    → Treatment:Involves surgical excision, additional therapies such as radiation therapy.
